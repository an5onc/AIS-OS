## Proposal PDF Rules

- Cover: blue/cyan gradient, NexGen logo above the title, green divider, white title text, contact block near the bottom.
- Inner pages: white header, NexGen logo top-left with breathing room, proposal title top-right, thin border line under header.
- Footer: NexGen Studio, website, email, phone, page number.
- Avoid placing a dark rectangle behind the logo. If the logo is faint, clean the logo mask instead of adding a box.
- Keep the PDF professional and service-business friendly. The full robot-head card can be used as source branding, but the proposal itself should be quieter.
