## Page Plan

### 1. Home Page

The homepage should quickly explain who The Last Brush LLC is, what kind of painting work you do, and how someone can request an estimate.

Recommended sections:

- Hero section with business name, short value statement, and "Request an Estimate" button.
- Service overview for interior painting, exterior painting, touch-ups, staining, cabinet painting, or other services you confirm.
- Trust section for insured/licensed status, years of experience, warranty, reviews, or customer satisfaction promise if available.
- Testimonials or recent work preview if you have customer quotes or project photos.
- Contact call-to-action.

### 2. About / Services Page

This page should make the business feel personal and credible while also giving search engines clear service information.

Recommended sections:

- Short company story.
- Mission or service promise.
- List of painting services.
- Service area.
- Process overview: estimate, prep, paint, walkthrough, cleanup.
- Why customers should choose The Last Brush LLC.

### 3. Contact Page

This page should remove friction for a potential customer.

Recommended sections:

- Contact form.
- Phone number and email.
- Map or service-area note.
- Social media links.
- Short "what happens after you submit" note.
