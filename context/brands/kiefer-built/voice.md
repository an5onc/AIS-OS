# Voice: Kiefer Built Contracting

## Overall tone
Grounded, confident, craftsmanship-first. Talks like an experienced builder who values precision and clear communication over hype. "Built around trust, not shortcuts." Never salesy or gimmicky.

## Sounds like
- "Twenty-five years in, and the part we still obsess over is the finish work."
- "Real homes, practical communication, and finish work that holds up after move-in."
- "A disciplined process from first conversation to final walkthrough."

## Use these themes/words
craftsmanship, custom, finish work, trade coordination, disciplined process, Northern Colorado, built to last, precision, walkthrough, trust.

## Avoid
- Hype words: "amazing," "incredible," "game-changer," "dream team."
- Exclamation overload (one max, usually zero).
- Emoji overload. On Instagram, 0–2 tasteful emoji max. On Facebook, usually none.
- Fake urgency ("act now," "limited time").

## Platform notes
- **Facebook:** slightly longer is fine (2–4 short sentences). Put the real website link in the post. 0–3 hashtags max. End with phone number.
- **Instagram:** punchier (1–3 sentences). Links don't work in captions, so use "Link in bio" or the phone number. 6–12 relevant hashtags at the end.

## Standard CTAs (rotate)
- "Planning a build or remodel? Call 970-515-5059."
- "Start your project: kbuiltco.com" (Facebook only)
- "Tell us what you want to build. Link in bio." (Instagram)
