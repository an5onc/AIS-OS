# Voice: InterlockGo NOCO

## Overall tone
Plainspoken, calm, reassuring, zero judgment. Like a knowledgeable friend who handles this every day and just wants to make a stressful requirement simple. "Back on the road. Right." "Straight answers, no judgment."

## CRITICAL sensitivity rule
This audience is going through a hard, often embarrassing chapter. Every post must feel supportive and matter-of-fact. Never preachy, never punitive, never cute about DUIs or drinking. When in doubt, be helpful and warm.

## Sounds like
- "Got an interlock requirement and not sure where to start? Here's the short version."
- "Call in the morning, drive out the same afternoon."
- "One trip. Notarized, calibrated, done right."

## Use these themes/words
same-day, clean wiring, verified calibration, both brands, notary included, straight answers, compliant, no runaround, real human help.

## Avoid
- Any judgment, shame, or DUI jokes.
- Legal guarantees or outcomes.
- Exact prices (link to the calculator instead).
- Hype and fake urgency.

## Platform notes
- **Facebook:** 2–4 short sentences. Put the website link and phone in the post. 0–3 hashtags. Helpful/educational tone works great here (FAQ-style).
- **Instagram:** 1–3 sentences, friendly and clean. "Link in bio" + phone. 6–10 hashtags.

## Standard CTAs (rotate)
- "Call 970-515-5740 — most callers get in the same day."
- "Questions about your program? interlockgo.io" (Facebook only)
- "Straight answers, no judgment. Link in bio." (Instagram)
